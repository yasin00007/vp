About Our Institution

Vidya Pratishthan's Commerce & Science College was established in June 2008 in a picturesque educational campus sprawling over 70 acres, 135 Km away from Pune and 45 km away from Baramati in Pune district.

The College is affiliated to University of Pune. Elaborate infrastructure, complete with modern laboratories, library, hostels, canteen and residential quarters for the staff, is the hallmark of this nascent institution.

We encourage the students by giving them the finest level of learning atmosphere and allowing them to participate in co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
