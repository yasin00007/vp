Welcome to VPCSC, Indapur!!!


With changing global scenario in education system, it is high time to reform entire teaching learning process which in turn helps in developing smart individuals. With the advancement in technology, learning now become more enjoyable & student friendly. Beside traditional set of learning, one can adapt different ways for acquiring skills that meet your needs. One such tool is MOOC (Massive Open Online Course). In India, one such initiative is SWAYAM, a program initiated by Govt. of India. It is designed to achieve three main principles of education policies namely viz; access, equity & quality.SWAYAM platform facilitates hosting of variety of courses to be accessed by anyone, anywhere at any time. All the courses are interactive, prepared by best teacher in the country & free of cost to any learner. To ensure academic excellence, we at VPCSC adapt innovative learning methodologies such as Smart Class Room, ICT enabled Lab, Innovation & Start-up Cell, MOODLE Learning Platform, Language Lab etc. Our student needs to focus on soft skills, entrepreneurial communication skills. We impart need based training in student to inculcate entrepreneurial spirit, enhance communication skills & aptitude test skills beside their regular curriculum.At VPCSC, we adapt a novel teaching-learning platform using MOODLE.
